# macOS Installation Guide

[简体中文](./macos-install.md) | English

This guide is for the first launch on macOS when `CodexLens.app` is blocked by Gatekeeper and macOS shows an "Apple cannot verify" warning.

## Steps

### Step 1: Click "Done" on the first warning dialog

When you open `CodexLens.app` for the first time, macOS may show a warning saying the app was not opened.
Do not click "Move to Trash". Click "Done" first.

![Step 1: Click Done](./assets/macos-install-step1.png)

### Step 2: Open "Privacy & Security" and click "Open Anyway"

Go to `System Settings` -> `Privacy & Security`, scroll to the `Security` section, find the blocked `CodexLens.app`, and click `Open Anyway`.

![Step 2: Click Open Anyway in Privacy & Security](./assets/macos-install-step3.png)

### Step 3: Confirm by clicking "Open Anyway"

macOS will show one more confirmation dialog. Click `Open Anyway`.

![Step 3: Confirm and open anyway](./assets/macos-install-step2.png)

After step 3 is completed, the app will ask for folder access permission on launch. Click `Allow`.
